Foundational Principles?

Starting from beginner level, students will learn the fundamental principles of Tajweed, including proper pronunciation, articulation points, and rules of recitation, laying a solid groundwork for advanced study.

Advanced Application ?

As students progress, they delve into more complex Tajweed rules and nuances, refining their recitation skills to achieve mastery and fluency in Quranic recitation.
